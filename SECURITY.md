## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report any security vulnerabilities to mahatma.mahardhika@programinglive.com.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for a fix. Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until it has been addressed.
